About

RM Shaw builders opened 5 years ago with the ethos of high quality service and unique workmanship.

This positive approach is illustrated though our building work and even more so in our stone mason division.

I have 30 years of experience in the building industry and throughout that time have strived to make sure the job is completed to both the clients and my own satisfaction. A builders skill is essential, however so is the desire to treat the customer fairly.

I have chosen the best tradesman to come with me and work for my company, as part of my approach, was to handpick the right people who match my view of what RM Shaw builders should be.

Quality, reliability and a strong work ethic, with the client at the forefront is the company’s mission statement.

You are welcome to contact me to taste test our aims.
